Stock market prices tend to change very fast.


So, if we can find the reasons on the basis of which the prices are changing, and if we can predict the approximate future price, we can generate higher profits.


Stock prices depend on a number of factors like market sentiment, company decisions, politics of the country, economic environment of the country, pumping and dumping and many more.


Also from the past changes over a period of time in the price of particular stock, we can get an idea of how the stock reacts to a particular condition.
